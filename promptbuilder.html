LLM Prompt Builder (Best Practices MVP)

Build a high-quality prompt by specifying role, task, audience, context, constraints, format, tone, examples, and quality checks. This is tool-agnostic and works for ChatGPT, Copilot-style chats, Claude, Gemini, local LLMs, etc.

Tip: Even a short example dramatically improves outputs.
Best-practice reminder: Strong prompts define the role, the task, the audience, the inputs, the required format, and constraints. If you want higher reliability, add success criteria and require the AI to list assumptions or ask clarifying questions.
